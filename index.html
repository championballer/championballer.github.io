Shiv Kumar

I am a senior under graduate student at Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, Delhi University where I am completing my Bachelor's in Engineering degree in the broad field of Information Technology.

In my time at college, I have studied and worked in many different fields of Robotics, Autonomous Traversal, Reinforcement Learning, Opportunistic and Delay Tolerant Networks, Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning through interships and projects. I love working on projects which are at the intersection of these fields. I have a deep liking for applied mathematics and am working daily in small chunks to satisfy my appetite.
